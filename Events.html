2nd Workshop

In October 2023, the second project workshop was held at IUT Longwy, bringing together the project partners for scientific exchange and progress review. During this workshop, the consortium members presented their latest research results and discussed ongoing developments related to vehicle modeling, intelligent estimation, autonomous driving, and experimental validation. This event provided an important opportunity to strengthen collaboration within the consortium, assess the progress of the different work packages, and further align the project’s research activities with its overall objectives. The workshop also reflected the project’s emphasis on regular scientific meetings and result dissemination throughout its duration.

Memories from the workshop.

Following the workshop activities, project members delivered a series of research presentations to share recent progress and highlight ongoing work within the consortium. These presentations covered key topics of the project, including vehicle modeling, intelligent estimation, observer design, connected autonomous vehicle applications, and experimental validation. Together, they illustrated the scientific advances achieved by the team and reflected the active exchange of ideas that supported the development of the project.
